## Evidence Note
Therapeutic peptides represent the forefront of drug discovery, offering potent and safe alternatives to traditional small molecules. However, their weak and context-dependent nature complicates the efficient virtual screening and structural characterization of protein–peptide patterns. Here we introduce RAPiDock, a diffusion generative model designed for rational, accurate and rapid protein–peptide docking at an all-atomic level. RAPiDock efficiently reduces the sampling space by incorporating physical constraints and uses a bi-scale graph to effectively capture multidimensional structural information while balancing efficiency. In addition, the model uses a Clebsch–Gordan tensor product-based architecture to ensure physical symmetry.
