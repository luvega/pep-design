## Evidence Note
The expansion and potentiation of regulatory T cells (Treg cells) offer an appealing approach for achieving immune tolerance and controlling overactive immune responses across a range of diseases. Current therapeutic approaches to expanding and improving function of Treg cells have predominantly relied on protein-based biologics, such as recombinant interleukin-2 (IL-2); in contrast, peptide-based interventions that can expand Treg cells with improved functional stability, enhanced specificity, and favorable safety profiles remain underexplored. Here, using a deep learning model, we identified a hexapeptide (DLST-6P) that preferentially expands both human and murine Treg cells while preserving their stability.
