## 输入

GPCR structures/epitopes, RFdiffusion and metaproteomic scaffold generation, display screening

## 输出

miniprotein GPCR agonists and antagonists

## 模型/工具

RFdiffusion, MetaGen/metaproteomic scaffolds, display and pharmacology screens

## 实验验证

experimentally validated binders, cellular pharmacology and selected in vivo/PK experiments

## 结构证据
